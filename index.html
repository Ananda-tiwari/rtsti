CONFERENCE BACKGROUND AND OBJECTIVES

Pashchimanchal campus is a constituent campus under the Institute of Engineering (IoE) of Tribhuvan University, Nepal. It has been running 7 different disciplines in Bachelor level including Geomatics Engineering and 5 masters programs whereas Rock and Tunnel Engineering is a new program in Nepal. It lies in Pokhara Metropolitan city a biggest and panoramic city of Nepal about 200 km west from the Kathmandu valley. It has 6 departments, Consultancy service, Liaison service, Center for space science and Geomatics studies, Research Management Unit as the functional units.
